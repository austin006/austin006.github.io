## Overview

With a teammate, I reverse engineered a **foamboard F-16 profile jet**. We built and flew the plane as part of the [BYU Aeronautics Club first-semester planes program](https://sites.google.com/view/byu-aeronautics/fixed-wing/first-semester-planes).

## Timeline

- **Duration:** September 2023 - December 2023
- **Total time:** 20 hours
- **Time commitment:** About 1 hour per week
