## Overview

I designed, built, launched, and safely recovered a rocket using an **H-class motor**. In doing so, I earned my **Level 1 certification** through the [Tripoli Rocketry Association](https://www.tripoli.org/).

This project expanded on the skills I developed from my earlier [G-level rocket project](/portfolio/2023/g-rocket/).

{{< callout title="Certification Context" variant="info" >}}
I completed this personal project as a member of [BYU Rocketry](https://rocketry.byu.edu/). The successful launch and recovery earned my [Tripoli Level 1 certification](https://www.tripoli.org/content.aspx?page_id=22&club_id=795696&module_id=468544).
{{< /callout >}}

## Key Contributions

- Designed and built a high-power rocket for Level 1 certification
- Completed a successful launch, recovery, and certification process
- Applied and extended rocketry skills from earlier student launch projects
