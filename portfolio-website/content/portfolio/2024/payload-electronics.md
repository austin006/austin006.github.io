## Overview

As a member of the [BYU Spacecraft Club](https://spacecraft.byu.edu/), I worked closely with a BYU Rocketry team to design a payload for their rocket. As one of five payload-team members, I contributed by **setting up an altimeter** and connecting it with other electrical components in the system.

One of the biggest accomplishments was sending a **camera in the payload** to record in-flight footage.

## Timeline

- **Duration:** January 2024 - April 2024
- **Total time:** 35 hours
- **Time commitment:** About 2 hours per week
