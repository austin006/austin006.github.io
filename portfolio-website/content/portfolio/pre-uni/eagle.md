## Overview

For my Eagle Scout project, I helped St. Mary's Church in England record gravestone information. We created **paper and digital records**, including pictures, for over **500 graves** that had previously been unrecorded.

This information was later published online to make the records searchable for family-history research.

## Timeline

- **Duration:** April 2017
- **Total time:** 129 hours contributed by me and the volunteers involved
