## Overview

I participated in NASA's [High School Aerospace Scholars](https://www.nasa.gov/learning-resources/high-school-aerospace-scholars/) program, a STEM learning experience designed to engage students with NASA missions.

I completed a five-month online learning phase followed by a five-day virtual summer camp. Because of COVID-19, I was unable to attend the planned residential experience at NASA's Johnson Space Center.

## Timeline

- **Duration:** October 2019 - April 2020
- **Total time:** 30 hours
